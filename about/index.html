My work has been published by top conferences (CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, AAAI, SIGGRAPH etc) and successfully applied to a number of important games (Justice PC, Ghosts PC, Ghosts Mobile, Revelation PC, Knives Out PC etc) in NetEase.
