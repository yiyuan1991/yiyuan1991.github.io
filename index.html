              Yi Yuan (袁 燚)

              AI Researcher at Netease Fuxi AI Lab

              About Me

              I am currently a senior artificial intelligence researcher and director of the Computer Vision Group at Netease Fuxi AI Lab as of 2017 after receiving my PhD from Wuhan University. My research interests include 3d face/body/object reconstruction, expression/motion generation and differentiable renderer etc. I focused on frontier research and technology transformation of computer vision and computer graphics in online games. My work has been published by top journals/conferences (TPAMI, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, AAAI, SIGGRAPH etc) and successfully applied to a number of important games (Justice PC, Ghosts PC, Ghosts Mobile, Revelation PC, Knives Out PC etc) in NetEase.

              I am hiring interns.
