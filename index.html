              Yi Yuan (袁 燚)

              AI Researcher at Netease Fuxi AI Lab

              About Me

              I am currently a senior artificial intelligence researcher and director of the Computer Vision Group at Netease Fuxi AI Lab (opens new window) as of 2017 after receiving my PhD from Wuhan University (opens new window). My research interests include 3d face/hair/body/object/scene reconstruction, expression/motion generation and differentiable renderer etc. I focused on frontier research and technology transformation of computer vision and computer graphics in online games. My work has been published by top journals/conferences (TPAMI, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, AAAI, SIGGRAPH etc) and successfully applied to a number of important games (Justice PC (opens new window), Ghosts PC (opens new window), Ghosts Mobile (opens new window), Revelation PC (opens new window), Knives Out PC (opens new window) etc) in NetEase.

              I am hiring interns.
